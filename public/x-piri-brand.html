Meet Piri

A payment app so sweet, people fall in love with the brand before they even open the QR. One scan, every way to pay.

Origin: Piri grew up on the corner of every barrio, watching vendors juggle Cash App, Venmo, three crypto wallets, and a prayer. Ridiculous.

Mission: Every payment method is a flavor. One scan. Pick yours. No drama.

Personality: Colorful, joyful, the life of the block. Sweet but never soft — Piri will advocate for you getting every coin you're owed.

Soul: Like the piragüero's cart — humble setup, but you'd walk across town for it.

Typography & Logo

Display: Fredoka One — friendly, rounded, bold — same energy as the character

Body: Nunito — warm, readable, human

Logo: piri (lowercase) — friendly lockup

Outline weight: thick dark brown (#2D0A00), never black

Brand Rules

  • Always use Fredoka One for the wordmark
  • Outline weight: thick dark brown (#2D0A00), never black
  • Piri is always lowercase in the logo
  • Flavors map 1:1 to payment methods — never break this
  • Background is always warm cream #FFFBF2 or a flavor color
  • Never use generic blue or purple gradients
  • Never use Inter, Roboto, or system fonts in brand materials

Flavors = Payment Methods

Each syrup flavor maps to a payment rail. Add a payment method in the app → unlock that flavor. The more methods, the more colorful Piri gets.

  • Fresa (#FF6B9D) — Ethereum
  • China (#F59E0B) — Bitcoin
  • Guayaba (#86198F) — Solana
  • Coco (#7BC8F5) — Base
  • Lima limón (#10B981) — Cash App
  • Frambuesa azul (#0EA5E9) — Venmo
  • Uva (#C97BF5) — Zelle
  • Tamarindo, Parcha, Piña, Cereza — reserved for future

Rainbow Goal: Connect all 7 payment methods = unlock Rainbow Piri. All layers lit up, cherry glowing, special animation plays. This is the app's north star engagement mechanic.
