# How FU Pay Me works

Quick version of the flow.

**If you’re getting paid:** You come to the app and create a profile—sign up, add your ETH, SOL, BTC (and other chain) addresses, plus Cash App, Venmo, Zelle. I generate one QR for everything.

**If someone’s paying you:** They scan your QR. They see your payment page, pick fiat or crypto, and either get sent to the right app with details pre-filled or connect their wallet. After they pay, I prompt them to make their own profile and QR so they can get paid the same way.
