# 🐛 Report a bug

Thanks for helping make Piri Pay better. This form takes about **1 minute**. We read every report.

---

## Quick form (≈1 min)

*Your engineer can replace this section with a real form that submits to your backend, Google Form, or GitHub Issues. Below are the suggested fields and placeholders.*

---

### 1. What went wrong? *(required, 1–2 sentences)*

*Example: "When I tapped Pay with Solana, the page went blank and never came back."*

**Field suggestion:** Short text area, placeholder: *Describe what happened in 1–2 sentences.*

---

### 2. What did you expect? *(required, 1 sentence)*

*Example: "I expected the wallet popup to open so I could sign the payment."*

**Field suggestion:** Short text input, placeholder: *What should have happened?*

---

### 3. Where were you? *(optional)*

- [ ] Paying someone (I scanned their QR / opened their link)
- [ ] Setting up my profile (creating QR / adding addresses)
- [ ] Viewing my QR / profile
- [ ] Other: ___________

**Field suggestion:** Single choice + optional “Other” text.

---

### 4. Device & browser *(optional but helpful)*

*Example: "iPhone 14, Safari" or "Chrome on Windows"*

**Field suggestion:** Short text input, placeholder: *e.g. iPhone Safari, Chrome Windows*

---

### 5. Contact *(optional)*

*If you’re okay with us following up for more details.*

**Field suggestion:** Email or username (optional).

---

## After you submit

We’ll use your report to fix issues and improve the app. If you left contact info and we need more detail, we’ll reach out. No spam, ever.

---

## Alternative: GitHub Issues

If the app links to a public repo, you can also open an issue with the same info:

- **Title:** Short summary (e.g. “Solana pay screen goes blank”)
- **Body:** What happened, what you expected, and (optional) device/browser and contact.
