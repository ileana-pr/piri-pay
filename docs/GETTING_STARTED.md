# 🍧 Get started with Piri Pay

**The short version:** You get one link and one QR. You add your payment info once. Everyone else uses that one link to pay you—Venmo, Cash App, crypto, whatever they like.

---

## 🎯 What is Piri Pay in one sentence?

**One page where all your ways to get paid live.** No more handing out five different link or usernames. One QR, one link, every option.

---

## 👤 I want to **get paid**

### Step 1: Open the app and sign up

Go to the app (e.g. **piri-pay.vercel.app**). Create an account with your email or wallet—then you’re ready to set up your profile.

### Step 2: Create your profile

- Tap **Create profile** (or **Get my QR**).
- Add how you want to get paid:
  - **Fiat:** Cash App, Venmo, Zelle (your username or cashtag).
  - **Crypto:** Your wallet addresses for Ethereum, Base, Solana, Bitcoin (paste from your wallet app).
- You don’t have to fill every option—only the ones you use.

### Step 3: Get your link and QR

- The app gives you **one link** and **one QR code**.
- Share that link or QR anywhere: socials, poster, sticker, bio.
- When someone opens it, they see **all** your payment options on one page and pick how they want to pay.

### Step 4: You’re done

- They pay you via their chosen app or wallet.
- You don’t have to do anything else. Money goes to the addresses/handles you added.

---

## 💸 I want to **pay someone**

### Step 1: Open their link or scan their QR

- Use the link they shared, or scan their QR with your phone camera.

### Step 2: Choose how to pay

- You’ll see one page with their options: Cash App, Venmo, Zelle, ETH, SOL, BTC, etc.
- Tap the one you want to use.

### Step 3: Pay

- **Fiat:** You’ll jump to Cash App / Venmo / Zelle with their info already filled in. Confirm there.
- **Crypto:** Connect your wallet (MetaMask, Phantom, etc.), enter amount, sign. Done.

---

## 📱 Tips


✅ Double-check addresses when you add them - crypto sent to the wrong address can’t be undone

✅ Add as many payment options as possible - more Piri flavors means more ways to get paid.
